Class name: Facility
URIhttp://www.epos-ip.org/term#Facility
LabelFacility
DescriptionThis class refers to facilities with resources and related services used by the scientific community to conduct top-level research in their respective fields, ranging form social sciences to astronomy, genomics to nanotechnologies. Examples include laboratories, special habitats, libraries,databases, biological archives[..]. A type of facility could be a research infrastructure a highter aggregated entity.
